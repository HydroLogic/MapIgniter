
==== System Requirements ====

  * [[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuGIS|UbuntuGIS]] / [[http://wiki.debian.org/DebianGis|DebianGIS]]
  * [[http://mapserver.org|MapServer 6.0+]]
  * [[http://httpd.apache.org|Apache Web Server 2.0+]]
